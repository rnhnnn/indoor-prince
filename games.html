    The Meeting Place

    You are Silvie, a resourceful fixer working for the renowned bard Joan of Castalen. Tasked with preparing an old cabin for a crucial meeting between powerful professors of magic, you must navigate hidden secrets, brewing tensions, and the magic that lingers in the air. As you uncover the cabin's mysteries, you’ll find that nothing is quite as it seems.
